About

If you are in the Middle East, you already know the difficulty in building a sustainable tech business. Even till this day, I face what is termed as VUCA (Volatility, Uncertainty, Complexity and Ambiguity) on a daily basis.

Nawatt was able to work on interesting projects worldwide for several universities, research centers, public agencies and companies including the European Space Agency, The Oklahoma department of Transportation, the Jabal Omar Development Company in Saudi Arabia and AlNaboodah holding group in Dubai.

Our Enterprise Asset Management system was a runner up in the 2018 best new technology category award in one of the UAE's most prominent business competitions.

Nawatt's core value is developing simple solutions for real world local problems using technology. For example, the first project we developed was a low cost custom made ultrasound medical scanner. You can see the crude design below.

Since I was a kid, I had a deep fascination of technology. This later materialized in specializing in Embedded Systems (Internet of Things) and Digital Signal Processing. I studied with great professors including Dr. Mohammed Adnan AlAlaoui at the American University of Beirut and Dr. Lina Karam at Arizona State University.
